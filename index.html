About the Competition

The North Carolina Physics Olympiad (NCPhO) is a monthly competition designed to challenge understanding of physics concepts and problem-solving abilities.

Format

Online, timed competition with 25 multiple choice questions.

Eligibility

Open to all students who are physics enthusiasts.

Rules

  • Individual competition (no team submissions)
  • Calculator allowed
  • No internet access during the exam

Scoring

  • 4 points awarded per correct answer for the first 20 questions
  • 6 points awarded per correct answer for the last 5 questions
  • 1 points awarded for a blank answer
  • No points for incorrect answers.

Prizes

Top performers receive certificates and recognition. Winners are featured on our leaderboard!
